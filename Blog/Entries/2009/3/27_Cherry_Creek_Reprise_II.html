Cherry Creek Reprise II

Friday, March 27, 2009

 

After the hill climb we forged on a little further and took a break to enjoy the views. Here is Steve getting his camera out to take a picture


View from the trail


Trying to decide which way to go. Straight ahead the trail ends so we decide to go left.


We ended up running into a fair amount of snow



If you look closely, you can see Matt’s jersey is ripped open in the front. He took a little dive over the handlebars in the snow...


We found some nice single track


But then lost it in the snow


We finally emerged in what Matt described as the “Shire”


Which included these interesting holes filled with ice


This is Cherry Creek Reservoir. First time I’ve been here.


Steve took this picture while on top of the dam


And here is the outlet at the base of the dam. Interesting seeing the ice buildup and water spraying out of holes in the outlet pipe.


Eric getting ready to lead us back to the truck


The trail is in the far left of the picture starting just above the seat of my bike (the one on the left)


After a fun high speed ride down whooped out trails and a gravel road it was time to load up and head home. Here is Matt at the end of the ride. Notice the rip in his jersey reveals “Honda” trying to bust out. Perhaps he has a Honda in his future?


It was another great day of riding, and an excellent workout. We rode for 4 hours and my heart rate monitor said I burned 2,550 calories. What a sport--lot’s of fun, beautiful back country to see, great friends to ride with, and excellent exercise!
