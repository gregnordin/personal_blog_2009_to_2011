5-Mile Pass: 1st dirt bike ride of 2009

Thursday, March 12, 2009

 

Finally, enough snow has melted to get the dirt bike out and ride! Matt, Steve, and I went to 5 Mile Pass for a few hours of afternoon riding. The temperature was in the low 60’s and the sky was blue--perfect riding weather! I’m including a few pictures below:


Mine tailing


After going up Rattlesnake Canyon we headed up Constrictor Canyon, the most interesting feature of which is this 6-7 foot waterfall that jeepers love to try and go up. We stopped before this point and walked the last little bit to show Matt, and to scope out a way around for a future attempt.


Just above where we left the bikes was this old mine carved into the side of the canyon.
